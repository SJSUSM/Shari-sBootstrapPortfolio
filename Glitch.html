Used "Audacity" and 3 movie stills from the horror/ supernatural / thriller film called "Late Night with the Devil"(2023) by Colin and Cameron Cairnes. The movie "follows" the events of a late talk night show episode that “aired” on halloween night of 1977. Due to the nature of the movie, I attempted to portray the madness and confusion that the protagonist was going through.
